The Ceremony & Reception

We are getting married on June 3rd, 2017 at Mortgage Hall.

The ceremony begins at 4:00 PM, however it is recommended that our guests arrive at 3:45 PM in order to be seated in an orderly fashion. There is ample parking at the venue! The reception will begin at 4:30PM at the same location and the reception officially ends at 10PM. There will be food, desserts, drinks, dancing and a generally great time.
