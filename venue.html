The Venue & Area

The Ceremony

We are getting married on 24th February 2017 at St Edmunds RC Church.

The ceremony begins at 12:30 PM, however it is recommended that our guests arrive at 12:00 PM in order to be seated in an orderly fashion. There is ample parking around the venue, and all are welcome to attend!

The Reception

The reception is being held at Hengrave Hall.

The wedding breakfast will take place straight after the ceremony, with the evening reception kicking off at 7pm.

Accommodation


There is a wide selection of places to stay in and around Bury St Edmunds, and the reception is a 10-15 minute drive from the town centre.
Below are a few examples, all prices quoted are for two people in a double room for Saturday 24th February 2018 unless otherwise stated. Parking is available at all of them.
